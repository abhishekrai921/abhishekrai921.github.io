About Me

I am a passionate and driven Ph.D. research scholar in Geomatics Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee, where I also completed my M.Tech. in Geomatics Engineering in 2019. I hold a Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch.) degree from NIT Raipur, earned in 2017. My interdisciplinary background uniquely positions me to bridge the gap between geospatial technologies and built environment applications.

My research lies at the intersection of Spatial AI, Photogrammetry, Augmented Reality (AR), Computer Vision, and Deep Learning, with a strong emphasis on practical, scalable applications. I am particularly focused on advancing technologies in:

  • Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM)
  • Indoor Mapping & Navigation
  • AR/VR integration with geospatial systems
  • LiDAR data processing and analytics

Throughout my academic journey, I have actively contributed to national and international conferences, collaborated with experts in academia and industry, and remained committed to pushing the boundaries of innovation in the geospatial domain.

My goal is to develop intelligent spatial systems that enhance how we perceive, interact with, and interpret our physical environment. I am always open to exploring collaborative research, interdisciplinary projects, and knowledge exchange with fellow researchers, startups, and industry leaders.
